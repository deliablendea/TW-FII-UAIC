FAQs

IS CLOUD9 A CLOUD STORAGE SERVICE?

cloud9 is like a cloud management channel and it does not have any storage space. If you have multiple clouds, then you can add them to cloud9 to manage and transfer your files from one cloud to other clouds as long as there is enough free space on your destination cloud.

IS CLOUD9 SAFE TO USE?

There is no doubt that cloud9 is safe to use. It does use three methods to ensure the safety of your account and data. (1) 256-bit encryption is applied on data transmission. (2) The authorization system of cloud9 is based on OAuth, which is a standard authorization framework that enables third-party applications to build up a connection to cloud services without username and password required. (3) Your data and files will not be stored on the cloud9 server. They'll be still kept in the original cloud drives. cloud9 can only be allowed to access your cloud drive account with your own permission.

DOES CLOUD9 HAVE APPS FOR MOBILE DEVICES?

cloud9 does not have a separate app at present.

WHAT CLOUD SERVICES ARE SUPPORTED BY CLOUD9?

Currently, cloud9 supports integration with at least three major cloud storage providers: Dropbox, Google Drive, and Microsoft OneDrive. More services may be added in the future for enhanced flexibility.

DOES CLOUD9 STORE ANY FILES LOCALLY OR ON ITS OWN SERVERS?

No. cloud9 does not store your files on its servers. All your data remains within your connected cloud storage accounts. The app only facilitates access and operations with your permission.

IS FILE TRANSFER RELIABLE IF THE FILE IS SPLIT ACROSS CLOUDS?

Yes. cloud9 uses redundancy and fragmentation handling mechanisms to ensure that large files split across multiple clouds can be reassembled safely and accurately.

CAN CLOUD9 PREVIEW FILES?

Generally, cloud9 can preview documents, text and picture files.
